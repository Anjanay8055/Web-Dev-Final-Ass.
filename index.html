A Family Tradition

Since the beginning, The Golden Crust has been committed to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. What started with just four varieties of cakes and pastries has now expanded to over 20 distinct offerings, including our highly popular cheesecake loved by younger generations.

Our journey began when Mr. Arjun Jain traveled to London to master the art of baking. Today, his son Mr. Ashok proudly continues this legacy while embracing modern techniques and equipment.
